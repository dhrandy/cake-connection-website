About

Welcome to The Vanhoose Cake Connection!

We take great pleasure in showcasing our style of desserts, occasion cakes & wedding cakes. From simple butter cream with delicate flavors to fondant-covered confectionary masterpieces, we work with you to create dessert flavors and designs that inspire and meet your exact need.

My Story

After making my son's cake for his second birthday, I realized how exciting it was to create something so special for the ones that I love. With the encouragement of my friends and family, I decided to open my own at home bakery.

I've been baking and decorating cakes since 2004. I love what I do, which is creating beautiful cakes that bring smiles to people's faces for every occasion.

The Vanhoose Cake Connection is committed to serving you only the highest quality, made from scratch desserts, baked fresh to order with the goal of delivering customers the most amazing tasting, best looking confections around! I hope you enjoy your Moments as much as I enjoyed being a part of them.

My company is based on the belief that my customers' needs are of the utmost importance. My As a result, a high percentage of the business is from repeat customers and referrals.

I would welcome the opportunity to earn your trust and deliver you the best service in the industry.
